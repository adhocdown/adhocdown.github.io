{Haley Alexander Adams}

I am a third year PhD student in the Computer Science Program at Vanderbilt University. My research encapsulates the interactions between perception and action in virtual and augmented environments.

My current work analyzes navigation with holograms in augmented reality (AR) and the effect of cognitive development on perception in head-mounted displays (HMDs).
