ACM-W Student Chapters

Haley was a founding member and chair of both her undergraduate and graduate university chapters. At Vanderbilt University, she worked alongside a team of undergraduates to found the Women in Computing's (WiC's) first annual emerging technology conference (Emerge) in 2017. For this event, she provided her expertise in virtual reality, handling event logistics, and recruiting guest speakers (2013-2015, 2016-2017).
[ vu women in computing ]   [ emerge 2018 ]  

Google igniteCS

In 2015, Dr. Betsy Williams Sanders and Haley founded a coding camp for middle and high school girls, which was sponsored by gifts from Google Education's igniteCS program in 2015 & 2016. She designed the curriculum and managed a team of camp counselors as they instructed sessions for Python, Scratch, and Unity together (2015, 2016).
